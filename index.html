Blacklight: Retribution

Wage War with Retribution!!!

Blacklight: Retribution is a 2012 cyberpunk-themed first-person shooter video game developed by Zombie Studios and published by Perfect World Entertainment for Microsoft Windows, and developed and published by Hardsuit Labs for PlayStation 4. This game is the second game in the Blacklight franchise making it a sequel to the game Blacklight: Tango Down which was made and published by Zombie Studios for PC and Xbox 360 in 2010. Blacklight Retribution is well known for its HRV(Hyper Reality Vision) making the game very fast-paced and original. The game features huge customization options from Head to Toe having over 37+ Helmets, 20+ Body Armour, 20+ Leggings, and 73+ Body and Gun Camos. Every Armour and Helmet have their own stats so you can mix and match to see whats right for you. The Guns also have this large varieties to mix and match from including 100+ parts from muzzle to stock. Being a free to play game it has its micro-transactions, but recently HardSuit Labs has made everything purchasable with its in game currency known as GP. The game even has it's own zombies mode which is Waved styled kind of Call of Duty Zombies. This game mode was very popular upon release but recently has been given lack of attention.

Today, the game is well aged but is still being updated by HardSuit Labs they adding a lot new content by the end of January adding new camos, 9 new original helmets, and titles along with matching emblems. Its even common now to find the developers in open lobbies playing with their beloved players time to time. The game even has a big community with even official MLG Tournaments known as BCL (Blacklight Community League). These tournaments are seasonal with a mix up each tourney providing new rules and game mode restrictions. BCL is livestreamed on the official website, Youtube, and Twitch by Youtubers who are willing. Blacklight Retribution offers a lot of content for everyone and is easy to play. Anyone can pick the game up pretty fast and become a natural.
